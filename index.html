Four questions to get us talking.
Blue Ocean Imagine broadly — what could be
Wait, AI can do that now?
What have you seen AI do lately — a demo, an article, a competitor — that made you think: if we could apply that here, it would change everything?
The magic wand
If you could wave a wand and have one autonomous thing running 24/7 — not a chatbot, not a dashboard, but something that acts — what would it do?
Red Ocean Fix what's known — what already hurts
The persistent pain
What's one workflow that's been a pain for years — you've tried to fix it, maybe even bought tools for it — and it still sucks?
The visibility black hole
Where do you have zero visibility today — and it costs you money, risk, or speed?
Before we build
What makes it ready to spike.
  • A sharp problem statement — the one thing we're solving, in a sentence.
  • A clear user or agent flow — what actually gets done, step by step.
  • Defined inputs and output — what goes in, what comes back out.
  • Hard constraints — the non-negotiables: cost, speed, data, systems.
  • Success criteria — what we'd need to see to call the spike a win.
